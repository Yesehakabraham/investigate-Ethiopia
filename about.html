Investigate Ethiopia Expeditions is a dynamic and rapidly expanding travel agency in Ethiopia, dedicated to offering comprehensive services for both groups and individuals traveling to and from the country. Our mission at Explore Ethiopia Expeditions is to provide authentic and immersive travel experiences. Our team comprises well-trained and experienced tour managers, travel agents, guides, and drivers who are fueled by a deep passion for travel. With a focus on showcasing the rich tapestry of places, people, and customs, we ensure our customers are taken care of with professionalism and dedication. We prioritize safety, comfort, and overall satisfaction, aiming to create unforgettable and genuine travel memories. Our agency boasts specialized departments catering to diverse needs. Each department operates independently, committed to delivering top-notch services and fostering lasting relationships with our clients. Conveniently located in Addis Ababa, all our services and departments, including Operational, Reservation, Sales & Marketing, Inbound, Outbound, and Domestic, are housed under one roof. This integrated approach gives us a competitive edge in providing seamless and exceptional travel experiences.

Investigate Ethiopia Tours is a privately owned tour business founded and operated by young, visionary Ethiopians, with operations commencing in early 2016. Our mission at Discover Ethiopia Tours is to curate and facilitate travel experiences that foster cultural understanding and provide an authentic glimpse into the diverse beauty of Ethiopia. We specialize in providing tailored services within Ethiopia, aiming to offer each client a comprehensive and enriching travel experience at competitive rates. Our company prides itself on offering unique tours to some of the most captivating destinations in Ethiopia. Traveling in a group not only offers economic benefits but also provides opportunities to forge friendships and share experiences with fellow travelers. In addition to group tours, which are predominantly organized during peak seasons, we cater to individual travelers visiting Ethiopia for various purposes, be it business or leisure. An increasing number of clients choose Discover Ethiopia Tours as their preferred travel partner, as we offer a one-stop solution for all their travel needs to Ethiopia. From securing Ethiopian visas and airline tickets from clients' hometowns to arranging hotel accommodations, transportation, sightseeing excursions, and both domestic and international flight tickets, we strive to ensure a seamless and memorable travel experience for every client
